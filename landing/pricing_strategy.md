# World Cup Quant Pricing Strategy

## Revenue Goal

Target: **USD $10,000** during the World Cup 2026 campaign.

This should not depend on personal betting profit. The main business model is selling:

- betting intelligence
- model probabilities
- value gap analysis
- risk labels
- bankroll discipline notes
- Telegram alerts
- premium CSV/report access

## Core Positioning

Do not sell this as guaranteed picks.

Sell it as:

> Data-driven World Cup betting intelligence for people who want to compare price vs probability.

## What Actually Drives Sales

The model alone does not sell.

Sales come from:

- trust
- daily content
- clear examples
- transparent tracking
- simple explanations
- urgency during the tournament
- responsible positioning

## What Not To Promise

Never say:

- guaranteed wins
- risk-free betting
- beat the bookies
- make money easily
- sure picks

Use safer language:

- model signal
- possible value gap
- price vs probability
- risk-aware analysis
- educational betting intelligence
