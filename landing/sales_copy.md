## How It Works

1. The system loads World Cup matches, team ratings, and odds.
2. It converts odds into market-implied probabilities.
3. It estimates probabilities using an internal model.
4. It calculates the gap between market probability and model probability.
5. It labels potential value signals and risk levels.
6. It generates reports, CSV files, newsletter text, and Telegram alerts.
7. Every signal can be tracked for transparency.
