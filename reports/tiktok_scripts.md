# TikTok / YouTube Shorts Scripts

## Script 1

### Hook
My World Cup model found a possible market gap in Czech Republic vs Mexico.

### Simple Explanation
The market odds imply that **Czech Republic** has about 22.3% chance.

My model estimates the probability closer to 32.6%.

That creates a value gap of **10.3%**.

Risk label: **HIGH RISK**. Value does not mean certainty. It only means the price may be different from the model estimate.

Confidence score: **37.0%**. This score combines model probability, value gap, and risk penalty.
### Closing
This is not a guaranteed bet. It is just a data signal. The goal is to compare price vs probability, not gamble emotionally.

### Caption
Data signal for Czech Republic vs Mexico: Czech Republic shows a 10.3% value gap. #WorldCup2026 #SportsAnalytics #BettingData #FootballAnalytics
