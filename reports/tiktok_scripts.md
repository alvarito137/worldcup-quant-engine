# TikTok / YouTube Shorts Scripts

## Script 1

### Hook
My World Cup model found a possible market gap in Australia vs Turkey.

### Simple Explanation
The market odds imply that **Australia** has about 21.1% chance.

My model estimates the probability closer to 39.2%.

That creates a value gap of **18.1%**.

Risk label: **HIGH RISK**. Value does not mean certainty. It only means the price may be different from the model estimate.

Confidence score: **51.3%**. This score combines model probability, value gap, and risk penalty.
### Closing
This is not a guaranteed bet. It is just a data signal. The goal is to compare price vs probability, not gamble emotionally.

### Caption
Data signal for Australia vs Turkey: Australia shows a 18.1% value gap. #WorldCup2026 #SportsAnalytics #BettingData #FootballAnalytics
