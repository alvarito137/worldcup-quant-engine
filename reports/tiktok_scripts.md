# TikTok / YouTube Shorts Scripts

## Script 1

Hook: My World Cup model found a possible market gap in Argentina vs Austria.

The market implies Austria has about 16.7% chance based on the odds.

But my model estimates it closer to 27.3%.

That creates a value gap of 10.7%.

This is not a guaranteed bet. It is just a data signal. The goal is not to gamble emotionally, but to compare price vs probability.
